作品簡介 · Introduction

《踏聲樂章》以「低碳行動」為核心隱喻，觀眾的踩踏被轉譯為能量的聲光回饋。是一場關於「人、環境與能量」的共鳴實驗。

七台改裝腳踏車與中央的「概念樹」構成聲光互動場域，當觀眾開始踩踏，能量在空間中流動、轉譯為光與聲的脈動。每一次加速、每一段節奏，都是一次微小卻確實的能量生成行動。裝置以「低碳行動」為隱喻，將日常的踩踏行為轉化為藝術化的能量循環，讓人感知自身參與環境共生的節奏。燈光在中央樹幹間呼吸閃爍，彷彿自然在回應人的律動；聲音的層次隨速度變化交織成一場即興樂章。作品不僅是一件互動藝術，更是一種對永續未來的詩意想像——當藝術回歸生活的節奏，能否讓我們重新聽見世界的呼吸？

EchoCycle is an experiment in resonance—between humans, the environment, and energy.Seven modified bicycles and a central “Concept Tree” form an interactive field of light and sound. As participants pedal, motion becomes energy, and energy transforms into luminous pulses and musical textures. Every acceleration, every rhythm, becomes a subtle yet tangible act of creation. The installation takes “low-carbon action” as its metaphor, translating the everyday act of cycling into an artistic loop of regeneration. The lights within the Concept Tree breathe in response to collective movement, while the layered soundscape shifts with the riders’ pace, composing an ever-changing symphony of human presence. EchoCycle is more than an interactive artwork—it is a poetic vision of sustainability. When art reconnects with the rhythm of life, can we once again hear the breath of the world?

音樂設計 · Music Design

待機模式：在待機模式中，音樂與概念樹的「呼吸燈」對應，營造出大自然寧靜卻充滿生命力的氛圍。音樂中融入了台灣特有鳥類的鳴聲，使聆聽體驗更具在地特色與自然感。音樂中時而響起「學校鐘聲」的弦律，為環境增添一種熟悉而溫暖的記憶感。整體設計以 10 分鐘為一循環單位，不斷重複播放，帶來持續、柔和的氛圍。

騎乘模式：展覽共設置 7 台腳踏車，分別對應七種不同樂器。隨機一台腳踏車有民眾使用時，會立即與待機音樂做切換。每一台腳踏車可觸發樂器的三種速度（節奏）變化。隨著後輪轉速不同，概念樹與腳踏車底座的閃燈會與音樂同步加快或放慢。

Standby Mode: In standby mode, the music corresponds with the “breathing light” of the conceptual tree, creating a tranquil yet vibrant atmosphere reminiscent of nature. The soundscape integrates calls of endemic Taiwanese birds, giving the listening experience a distinctive local and organic quality. Interwoven within the composition are melodic fragments of “school bell chimes,” evoking a sense of warmth and familiarity. The entire sound design operates on a ten-minute looping cycle, continuously replaying to maintain a soft and immersive ambience.Cycling Mode: The installation features seven bicycles, each corresponding to a distinct musical instrument. When any one of the bicycles is in use, the system immediately transitions from standby music to cycling mode. Each bicycle can trigger three rhythmic variations of its assigned instrument. As the rear wheel’s rotation speed changes, the flashing lights on both the conceptual tree and the bicycle base adjust in sync with the tempo, visually and sonically reflecting the rider’s motion.

永續與再利用 · Sustainability

工業模組化尺寸、低度改裝腳踏車：展後可恢復使用；感測與線材可回收再用；鋁擠型可轉製為書櫃/置物架；玻璃+IKEA 桌腳可成一張好用的書桌。

Modular, low-impact design enables re-use: bikes revert to normal, sensors & cabling migrate to new builds, aluminum profiles become furniture.
