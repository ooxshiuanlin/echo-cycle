踏聲樂章｜EchoCycle

公共藝術聲音互動裝置 · 國立成功大學科技藝術碩士學位學程

作品簡介 · Introduction

《踏聲樂章》以「低碳行動」為核心隱喻：觀眾的踩踏被轉譯為能量的聲光回饋。每一台腳踏車如同一件樂器，既可獨奏亦可合奏；中央「概念樹」以燈光匯聚眾人能量，象徵人與環境的共振循環。作品結合 TouchDesigner 聲光資料流、Phidgets 感測、DMX 燈控與多聲道音訊，回應「在公共空間如何以藝術化方式理解永續」。

EchoCycle transforms human motion into a symphony of light and sound. Each bicycle acts as an instrument—solo or ensemble—while the Concept Tree responds to collective energy. Integrating TouchDesigner, Phidgets, DMX, and multi‑channel audio, the work explores how sustainability can be perceived through interactive art.

音樂設計 · Music Design

音樂概念：待機模式中，音樂與概念樹的「呼吸燈」對應，營造大自然寧靜卻充滿生命力的氛圍；融入台灣特有鳥鳴，增添在地與自然質地；間或響起「學校鐘聲」的旋律，帶入熟悉而溫暖的記憶。整體以 10 分鐘為循環單位，持續而柔和。騎乘模式下，7 台腳踏車對應七種不同樂器；任一腳踏車被啟動，系統即與待機音樂切換；每台可觸發三種速度（節奏）變化，後輪轉速將驅動概念樹與底座閃燈，與音樂同步加快或放慢。

Music Concept: In standby, the composition synchronizes with the Concept Tree’s breathing lights, evoking calm vitality. Native Taiwanese birdsong adds local, organic texture; a familiar school‑bell motif surfaces at times, bringing warmth and memory. The score loops every 10 minutes for a gentle continuum. In riding mode, seven bikes map to seven instruments; activation seamlessly switches from ambient to interactive. Each bike offers three rhythmic tiers; wheel speed modulates both the lights and the musical tempo.

永續與再利用 · Sustainability & Reuse

裝置採工業模組化設計，腳踏車僅低度改裝，展後可恢復使用；感測器與線材可於後續專案再利用；鋁擠型結構可再製為書櫃或置物架；玻璃搭配 IKEA 桌腳即可化為書桌。設計不僅概念上呼應低碳循環，也在材料生命週期上落實再利用。

Built on modular industrial standards, bicycles are minimally modified and revertible to daily use; sensors and wiring are reusable; aluminum profiles repurpose into furniture; glass panels pair with off‑the‑shelf legs to become desks. Circularity is enacted both as metaphor and material practice.
