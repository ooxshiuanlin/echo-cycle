作品簡介 · Introduction

《踏聲樂章》以「低碳行動」為核心隱喻，觀眾的踩踏被轉譯為能量的聲光回饋；每一台腳踏車如同一件樂器，既可獨奏亦可合奏，中央「概念樹」以燈光匯聚眾人能量，象徵人與環境的共振循環。 “EchoCycle” uses low‑carbon movement as its central metaphor. Pedaling is translated into responsive light and sound; each bicycle behaves like an instrument—solo or ensemble—while the Concept Tree gathers collective energy as a luminous symbol of human–environment resonance.

作品結合 TouchDesigner 資料流、Phidgets 感測、DMX 燈控與多聲道音訊，回應「在公共空間如何以藝術化方式理解永續」。 Integrating TouchDesigner dataflow, Phidgets sensing, DMX lighting, and multi‑channel audio, the work explores how sustainability can be encountered through interactive art in public space.

音樂設計 · Music Design

待機模式中，音樂與「概念樹」的呼吸燈對應，營造寧靜而充滿生命力的氛圍；樂音融入台灣特有鳥鳴與「學校鐘聲」旋律；整體以 10 分鐘為循環單位。 In standby, the music synchronizes with the Concept Tree’s breathing lights—calm yet alive—with Taiwanese native birdsong and a familiar school‑bell motif. The ambient score loops every 10 minutes.

騎乘模式下，七台腳踏車對應七種樂器；任一車被啟動即與待機音樂無縫切換。每台可觸發三段速度（節奏）變化，後輪轉速將同步影響概念樹與底座的閃燈。 In riding mode, seven bicycles map to seven instruments; activation switches seamlessly from ambient to interactive. Each bike exposes three rhythmic tiers; wheel speed modulates both the lights and musical tempo in sync.

永續與再利用 · Sustainability & Reuse

採工業模組化設計：腳踏車僅低度改裝，展後可恢復使用；感測器與線材可於後續專案再利用；鋁擠型結構可轉製為書櫃或置物架；玻璃搭配 IKEA 桌腳可化為書桌。 Built on modular industrial standards: bicycles are minimally modified and revertible; sensors and wiring are reusable across projects; aluminum profiles repurpose into shelving; glass panels pair with off‑the‑shelf legs to become desks.

設計不僅在概念上呼應低碳循環，也於材料生命週期落實再利用。 Circularity is enacted both as metaphor and as material lifecycle practice.
